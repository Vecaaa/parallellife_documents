ParallelLife ("the App") is developed with your privacy as a core principle. This policy explains what data the App handles and how.

1. No Account Required
ParallelLife does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information (such as name, email, or phone number) to use any feature of the App.

2. Data Storage and iCloud Sync
All the data you create in the App — tasks, calendar entries, focus sessions, custom items, attributes, achievements, wallpapers, and app settings — is stored locally on your device.

If you enable iCloud Sync in Settings, this data is synced across your own devices using Apple's CloudKit service, stored in your personal private iCloud container. This data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple, is never accessible to the developer, and is never shared with any third party. You can disable iCloud Sync at any time in the App's settings.

3. Photo Access
If you choose to set a custom wallpaper, the App uses Apple's system photo picker (PhotosUI) to let you select an image. The App does not have direct access to your full photo library — you only grant access to the specific photo you pick. The selected image is stored locally on your device (and synced via your own private iCloud container if iCloud Sync is enabled). It is never uploaded to any server operated by the developer or any third party.

4. In-App Purchases
ParallelLife offers an optional one-time purchase ("Max" membership) to unlock additional features. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple's App Store / StoreKit. The developer does not receive, process, or store your payment information (such as credit card numbers or billing details).

5. No Third-Party SDKs, Analytics, or Advertising
The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking SDKs. No usage data, behavioral data, or device identifiers are collected or transmitted to the developer or any third party. The App does not track you across other apps or websites.

6. Data Sharing
The developer does not collect, access, sell, or share any of your data with third parties, advertisers, or data brokers, because the developer has no server-side infrastructure that receives your data in the first place.

7. Children's Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect personal information at all.

8. Data Deletion
Since all data lives locally on your device and/or in your own private iCloud container, you can delete all App data at any time by deleting the App, or by disabling iCloud Sync and removing the App's data from iCloud in your device's Settings.

9. Changes to This Policy
This policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted at this same URL with an updated "Last updated" date.
